Correlations Discovered Between Income, Age and Health Risks

Poverty Correlations

  • Poverty vs. Obesity
    • As seen in graph, there is a weak positive correlation between the two.
  • Poverty vs. Smokes
    • There is a comparatively stronger negative correlation between the two.
  • Poverty vs. Lacks Healthcare
    • There is a weak positive correlation between the two.

Age Correlations

  • Age vs. Obesity
    • There does not seem to be any correlation between the two parameters.
  • Age vs. Smokes
    • There does not seem to be any strong correlation between the two parameters. However it is higher between the ages of 32 to 40.
  • Age vs. Lacks Healthcare
    • There is a weak negative correlation between the two.

Household Income Correlations

  • Household Income vs. Obesity
    • Contradictor to general assumption, there is negative correlation between the two parameters.
  • Household Income vs. Smokes
    • Not a very definitive pattern, but slight negative correlation.
  • Household Income vs. Lacks Healthcare
    • There is a slight negative correlation between the two.
